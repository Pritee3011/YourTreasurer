About YourTreasurer

YourTreasurer is a student-focused digital CFO app for budget tracking, loan accountability, recurring spend reminders, and visual analysis.

  • Guardian tier mails at 10%, 5%, and over-budget.
  • Loan handshake and one-click friend reminder flow.
  • Cloudinary receipt vault with URL storage in MongoDB.
  • Day/Month analytics charts from live aggregation APIs.
